Agency Automation Audit

A high-signal diagnostic for founder-led agencies to audit their systems and reclaim their focus.

1. Lead Follow-Up

New leads receive an automated touchpoint (Email/SMS) within 5 minutes.
Form submissions automatically create/update records in a CRM (no copy-pasting).
Team is notified instantly via Slack/Discord when a qualified lead books a call.
A multi-step nurture sequence triggers automatically for non-booked leads.

2. Client Onboarding

Signed contracts automatically trigger the creation of shared folders and Slack channels.
Onboarding questionnaires are sent automatically after the first payment is confirmed.
Client project boards are auto-populated with standard task templates based on the offer.
Initial "Welcome" video or doc is delivered without manual intervention.

3. Reporting & Admin

Data for weekly/monthly reports is aggregated automatically (no manual spreadsheet entry).
Recurring invoices are set to auto-pay or auto-generate without reminders.
KPI dashboards are live and accessible without a "reporting day" scramble.

4. Task Handoff

Meeting notes are automatically summarized and synced to the project management tool.
Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) are linked directly inside project task descriptions.
Dependencies between team members are managed via automated status triggers.

5. Founder Bottleneck

Founder calendar is 100% automated (no "Does this time work for you?" emails).
Email inbox is triaged by an agent or system, leaving only high-value decisions.
Routine approvals (expenses, minor creative) are handled via logic, not Slack pings.
