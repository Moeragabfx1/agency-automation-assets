Client Onboarding Automation Checklist

Use this to remove the usual first-week mess: missed handoffs, late kickoffs, scattered docs, and founder-driven babysitting.

1. Client Intake

1
Contract + payment trigger the workflow automatically.No manual "someone should start onboarding" message floating in Slack.
2
New client record is created in the CRM and PM tool.One source of truth. No duplicate copy-paste across tools.
3
Intake form is sent instantly.Access requests, goals, stakeholders, and assets collected before kickoff.

2. Internal Handoff

4
Owner, strategist, and delivery lead are assigned by rule.Not by whoever happens to notice the new client first.
5
Internal handoff notes are standardized.Offer sold, scope, risks, deadlines, and promised outcomes are visible in one place.
6
Slack/Discord channel and shared folders are auto-created.Clean workspace before the first call, not after the chaos starts.

3. Kickoff Scheduling

7
Kickoff invite is triggered from intake completion.Stop chasing clients for scheduling after they already paid.
8
Agenda and prep materials go out automatically.Clients show up knowing what is needed and who is involved.

4. Task Generation

9
Offer-specific task templates are applied automatically.Every onboarding starts from a proven sequence, not a blank board.
10
Dependencies and deadlines are preloaded.No founder needs to manually map the first two weeks every time.

5. Owner Visibility

11
There is a single dashboard for onboarding status.If a client is blocked, you should know before they ask.
12
Common failure points are flagged automatically.Missing intake, missing assets, no kickoff date, no owner, overdue tasks.

Onboarding should feel controlled.

If every new client creates internal confusion, the problem is not volume. It is the system.
