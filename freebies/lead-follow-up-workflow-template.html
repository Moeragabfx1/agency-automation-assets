Agency Operations

Lead Follow-Up Workflow Template

Standardizing the path from inbound interest to qualified appointment with zero leakage.

01. Trigger
New Inbound Lead
Lead source activation via Website Form, LinkedIn/Twitter DM, Direct Email, or Paid Ad Landing Page.
02. Timing
The 5-Minute Rule
Immediate auto-confirmation followed by personalized manual outreach (Voice Note or Loom) within 5 business minutes.
03. Qualification
Async Discovery
Verify Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline (BANT) via DM discovery or a short Typeform before commit.
04. Handoff
CRM Sync & Alerting
Qualified lead logged in CRM + Automated Slack/Discord notification to the Account Executive or Founder.
05. Follow-Up
The 6-Touch Loop
Standardized cadence over 30 days: Day 1, 2, 4, 7, 14, 30. Transition to "Closed-Lost" if no engagement.
06. Visibility
Daily Pipeline Audit
9:00 AM check ensuring every lead has an owner, a "Next Task" date, and an updated status.
Build systems, not just lists.
